Classical Architecture

Know about this style now

Classical Architecture corresponds to the architecture of Classical Antiquity, originated in Greece and later added by the Romans. Its main features are the ornate columns (Doric, Ionic and Corinthian) and the use of concrete and marble. Examples of buildings in this style are the Parthenon on the Acropolis of Athens and the Colosseum.
